Christopher Morris

Christopher is someone who is a lifelong learner who believes in continuous improvement. He is somewhat of a perfectionist and as determined as they come. Though his voice may be rough and he may appear to be somewhat serious at times, he is very approachable, down-to-earth, and possesses a heart of gold. His life's experiences has taught him to take nothing or no one for granted, as oftentimes it's the small, seemingly insignificant things that turn out to be the cornerstone. Righteousness and justice are principles he has always held close and he would rather to do the right thing than the popular thing. Most of the time he is a quiet observer, but put him in the right setting and he can ignite the conversation or atmosphere. He likes to teach and play pool when he finds spare time.
