Theme

A square colour scheme comprising of orange, yellow green, blue, and red purple was used throughout the website. A neutral cream yellow hue was used in areas with plenty text to give the nostalgic feeling of reading a book. The yellow green and orange were used as accent colours to add energetic pop to the pages. The red purple added a rich, softer touch. Blues were used in a monochromatic sense to give tranquility in some aspects and strong assurance in others.
My target audience is primarily prospective employers but would also be made available to anyone who wants to get to know me more.
